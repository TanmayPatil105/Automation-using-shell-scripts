Usage: wifi [OPTION] {Arguments}

Get status of Wi-Fi or turn it on/off.

wifi on		Turns wifi on
wifi off	Turns wifi off
wifi list	Lists all available wifi
wifi connect 	Connect to all available wifi  networks
"SSID"
wifi pass	To share password of connected network
wifi saved 	To display all the saved networks
wifi hotspot 	To turn on wifi hotspot
